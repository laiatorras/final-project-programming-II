Color psychology explores the profound impact colors exert on human emotions, behaviors, and perceptions.

For instance, warm tones like reds and oranges often evoke feelings of passion, energy, or urgency.

While cooler shades like blues and greens tend to induce calmness, tranquility, or even sadness.
